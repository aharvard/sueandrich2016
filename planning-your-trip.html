Planning Your Trip

Accommodations

We encourage out-of-town guests to utilize Airbnb.com for inexpensive and comfortable accommodation in the area. Neighborhoods nearby our residence and the wedding venues include: Poncey-Highland, Old Fourth Ward, Inman Park, Virginia-Highland, Candler Park, Edgewood, and Reynoldstown.

We’ll also be happy to direct you to any one of Atlanta’s multiple hotels, but point you specifically to The Highland Inn for its convenient location and modest price:

Please don’t hesitate to contact us for further guidance on where to stay!

Transportation

Atlanta is a great city to explore, and you have several options for ground transportation. Guests who arrive at the Atlanta airport can procure a rental car or taxi cab easily. Atlanta’s public transportation system (MARTA) offers bus and rail service, but may require planning ahead to ensure that accommodation is within walking distance of a station. Both the wedding and reception venue are within one mile of MARTA rail stations. Guests who are unable to drive should contact us personally for further guidance and to arrange transportation to wedding events.
